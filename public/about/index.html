About This Site

Karso Suryo Putro

The hope of creating this site is to fight those by creating this personal “digital garden”, thus i can be more thoughtful and intentional in consuming stuffs from the internet.

You may find my writings to be helpful, but you may not. If you do, you can connect to me through twitter/X or email. Maybe, just maybe, you feel relate to me and perhaps by connecting we can be friend.
