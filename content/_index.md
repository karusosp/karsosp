## [Blogs](/blog)
My mind is a mess and full of distraction. The reason for me to start blogging
is to reduce the amount of distraction that i get by consuming digital media.
You could say that it is a project of mine to systematize my mind. 

## [Essays](/essay)
To consume and memorize things is one thing, you could say it is a form of
passive learning. But i believe _true learning_, that is a learning process for
the purpose of **understanding**, can only be attain by crystalizing and
systemizing knowledge. The best way to crystalize knowledge is through writing
it down as eloquently as possible. These essays are my attempt to do just that.
