## [Blogs](/blog)
My mind is a mess and full of distraction. The reason for me to start blogging
is to reduce the amount of distraction that i get through consuming digital media.
You could say that this personal site is a personal project of mine for the
purpose of systemizing my mind through self-expression. With this i hope that i
can be more intentional in consuming media. 

## [Essays](/essay)
To consume and memorize things is one thing, you could say it is a form of
passive learning. But i believe _true learning_, that is a learning for
the purpose of **understanding**, can only be attained through crystalization and
systematization of knowledge. I believe there is no better way to crystalize knowledge 
than through writing it down. These essays are my attempt to do just that.
